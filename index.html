SAFETY TIPS FOR CANOEISTS

  1. Be able to swim
  2. Wear a life jacket at all the time
  3. Don't stand up or move around. If canoe tips,
    • Hang onto the canoe
    • Use the canoe to support and
    • swim to shore
  4. Dont overexert your self
  5. Use a bow light at night
